What is Drawing ?

Drawing is a form of visual expression where individuals create images or designs using various tools such as pencils, pens, markers, charcoal, or digital drawing software. It is a fundamental form of human communication and creativity, allowing individuals to convey ideas, emotions, and experiences visually.

How To Start Drawing

Children can potentially earn money from their drawing skills through various avenues:

  • Commissioned Artwork

    Children can offer their drawing services for commissioned artwork. This could involve creating custom illustrations, portraits, or designs for clients who are willing to pay for personalized artwork.

  • Art Contests and Competitions

    Many organizations, schools, and businesses host art contests and competitions that offer cash prizes or scholarships to winners. Children can participate in these contests to showcase their talents and earn money or recognition for their work.

  • Selling Art Online

    Children can sell their artwork online through platforms such as Etsy, Redbubble, or Society6. These platforms allow artists to upload their designs and sell them as prints, stickers, apparel, or other merchandise. Children can earn a commission for each sale made through their online store.

  • Art Classes and Workshops

    Talented young artists can offer art classes or workshops to teach others their drawing techniques. They can charge a fee for participation and share their skills with aspiring artists in their community.

Start Your journey Now

Children often start drawing at a very young age, usually as soon as they are able to hold a crayon or pencil. As they grow and develop their skills, some children may express interest in earning money through their drawings
