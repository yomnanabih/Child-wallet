Your Fifth Step is Find Opportunities

Look for auditions for voice-over roles in local theater productions, school projects, or online casting websites. You can also reach out to animation studios, audiobook producers, or advertising agencies to inquire about voice acting opportunities for children.

How to Find Opportunities?

Here's a child-friendly guide on how to find voice-over opportunities:

  • Search Online

    Look for websites and online platforms that list voice-over auditions and casting calls for kids. Some websites specifically cater to young voice actors, making it easier for you to find roles that are suitable for your age and skill level.

  • Talk to Your Parents and Teachers

    Your parents and teachers can be valuable resources in helping you find voice-over opportunities. They might know about local theater productions, school projects, or community events that are looking for young voice actors.

  • Join Voice Acting Communities

    Join online forums, social media groups, or local clubs dedicated to voice acting. These communities can provide support, advice, and information about upcoming auditions and opportunities in the voice-over industry.
