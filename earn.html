How To Start Learning.

Before beginning to study anything, it's crucial to ask yourself a series of questions to establish clear objectives and intentions :

  • why you are studying it ?
  • what benefits you hope to gain ?
  • what actions you can take afterward ?
  • when you will allocate time for studying ?

Once you have answered these questions, you can confidently embark on your learning journey.
