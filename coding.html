What is Coding ?

Coding, also known as programming, Coding is a list of step-by-step instructions that get computers to do what you want them to do. Coding makes it possible for us to create computer software, games, apps and websites. Coders, or programmers, are people who write the programmes behind everything we see and do on a computer

Coding involves using logical and structured thinking to create algorithms and programs that solve problems or accomplish goals.

Why Start Learn Coding

Kids who learn to code move from being passive consumers of technology to being active creators. Creation is not just about having ideas, it also requires the tools to bring that idea to life. By learning to code, they can put their ideas, talent, creativity, and interests into making their own technological solutions

Here are several reasons why starting to learn coding at a young age can be beneficial for children:

  • Prepares for the Future

    As technology continues to advance, the demand for individuals with coding skills is expected to grow. By learning to code at a young age, kids gain a competitive advantage in the job market and open up opportunities for future careers in technology-related fields.

  • Enhancing Digital Literacy

    Learning to code improves children's understanding of how digital technology works. It enhances their digital literacy skills, enabling them to navigate and use technology more effectively and responsibly.

  • Encouraging Creativity

    Coding empowers children to create their own digital projects, such as games, animations, and stories. It encourages creativity by allowing them to turn their ideas into reality through code.

  • Opening Doors to Various Career Paths

    Proficiency in coding opens up a wide range of career opportunities in fields such as software development, computer science, data analysis, and robotics. Starting to learn coding at a young age gives children a head start in building the skills needed for these careers.
