Guardians of the Galaxy

Join the Guardians of the Galaxy in this exciting comic about the importance of saving money, which teaches readers about wants versus needs. The collector's item financial literacy comic called "Rocket's Powerful Plan" has a fast-paced plot in which the characters learn the importance of saving and managing personal finances while defeating the evil Collector and Robo-Pests. The educational comic also includes a mini-game activity worksheet, finance terms, and more.
